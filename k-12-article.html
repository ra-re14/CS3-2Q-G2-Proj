From Kindergarten to Graduation: The Lifelong Impact of K-12 Learning Benefits

The K–12 system has been redefining education using technology, elevating it above academic achievement. What is the Purpose of the K12 Learning System?

Twelve years of basic education are referred to as the K12 learning system. Beginning in kindergarten and continuing through grade 12, it is an organized method of education. The foundation of a student's academic career is this educational system. Ensuring that children understand the fundamentals of education is the primary goal of the K–12 system. Students in this educational system are prepared for higher education, the workforce, and entrepreneurship.
